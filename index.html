There are 2 types of elements

1)Inline Elements; These are those elements which take up the space needed, if needed then they will go in other's space and will let others go in to their space.
2) Block Level Elements; These are those elements which will take the whole window's width, they will not go in others space and will neither allow any other tag to go in their space.

There are 2 types of tags

1)Structural Tags are those tags which are used for the structure of the website. -Example;bold or italic= These sound similar and normal in sound reader.

2)Semantic Tags are those tags which are used for style and design. -Example;em or strong= These are given emphasisand have a deep voice in sound reader.
